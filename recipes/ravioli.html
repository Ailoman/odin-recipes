Ravioli Recipe

A steaming hot serving of Ravioli

  1. Mix ricotta, garlic, herbs and lemon zest
  2. Fry spinach until collapse
  3. Consolidate spinach and mix, spread unto pasta patches
  4. Paint eggwhite along the edge of the patches, then fold a second patch on top of it and seal
  5. Boil until the pasta is tender, and serve
